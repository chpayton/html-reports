The cheapest real food for every nutrient

For each nutrient, which whole foods deliver it for the least money? Bars show the cost of one day's Daily Value: the price of eating enough of that one food to hit 100% of the FDA Daily Value. Lower is cheaper. Prices are US national averages in nominal dollars (USDA and BLS). Each food is shown at its cheapest available source, so the pricing date varies; hover any mark for its source and period.
